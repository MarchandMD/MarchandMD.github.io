Learning how to build and play is a lot of work

I mean, if it was easy, I guess everyone would be doing it. There certainly are a lot of people who are in technology, and the amount of people seems to be growing.

But it seems like there is more of a shift to performing work for others; corporations, companies, large organizations. But the entire internet exists for the purpose of sharing factual information. Everything else about it has been for amusement, and been advanced by people willing the internet to become what it is today.

And I've been alive for the entirety of the internet. At least the wider adoption of it. So it's still something that's new. Why not learn about it?!

But creating a personal website shouldn't be stressful; it shouldn't be difficult, and it shouldn't be critiqued too harshly. I mean, this thing exists as a "living lab"...a place to try things and learn things. Not for the sake of forgetting things, but for the sake of finding what works.
